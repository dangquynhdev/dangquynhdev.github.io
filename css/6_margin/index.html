CSS Margin Collapse

Top and bottom margins of elements (display:block) are sometimes collapsed into a single margin that is equal to the largest of the two margins.
This does not happen on left and right margins! Only top and bottom margins!
ví dụ:
div.block1 margin-bottom: 100px;
div.block2 margin-top: 20px
=> kết quả space margin là 100px
